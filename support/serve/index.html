Set Up Team

The Set Up Team is currently recruiting volunteers who are willing to aide in setting up on Sunday mornings before the service.

The Set Up Team works to prepare all equipment and supplies needed for Sunday mornings! We do this by:

  • Praying during the week that God will use our efforts to reach Herriman with His love!
  • Using Planning Center Online to accept/decline scheduling requests and to block out any upcoming unavailability.
  • Arriving at 8 am to begin the set up process.
  • Working together to set up all areas including Lobby, Kid’s Life, and Worship Area.

Kid's Life Team

*Note: All Kid’s Life Volunteers are required to pass a background check.

The Kid’s Life Team is currently recruiting for the following service roles:

  • Kid’s Life Birth-3 Teacher
  • Kid’s Life 4-Kindergarten Teacher
  • Kid’s Life 1st-5th Grade Small Group Leader

The Kid’s Life Team works to connect kids to Christ! We do this by:

  • Praying for our students and their families during the week.
  • Reviewing the prepared lesson before Sunday.
  • Using Planning Center Online to accept/decline scheduling requests and to block out any upcoming unavailability.
  • Arriving at 10 am Sunday morning to pray and prep with team.
  • Following the prepared lesson to provide a welcoming, fun, creative environment in which kids learn about Jesus!

1st Impressions Team

The 1st Impressions Team is currently recruiting for the following service roles:

  • Front Door Greeter
  • Tour Guide
  • Kid’s Life Registration
  • Welcome Center Greeter
  • Café Greeter

The 1st Impression Team works to make Lifestone the most welcoming, visitor-friendly church around! We do this by:

  • Praying during the week that new families find courage to visit Lifestone.
  • Using Planning Center Online to accept/decline scheduling requests and to block out any upcoming unavailability.
  • Arriving at 10 am Sunday morning to pray with volunteer team.
  • Warmly greeting each person/family, guiding guests through Kid’s Life Registration process, escorting guests as they find their way to Kid’s Life, Café, and Worship areas, being available to answer any questions they may have about Lifestone.

Audio/Visual Team

The Audio/Visual is currently recruiting for the following service roles:

  • Sound Board Mixer
  • Slides Presenter

The AV Team works to provide an optimal worship experience on Sunday mornings! We do this by:

  • Praying during the week that Sunday morning will be the perfect time and place for attenders to connect with God and with each other.
  • Using Planning Center Online to accept/decline scheduling requests and to block out any upcoming unavailability.
  • Arriving at 8 am to help with set up of the Worship Area and to rehearse with the band.
  • Working together to ensure that all elements of the service are carried out with excellence including music, lights, slides, and a seamless flow of service.
